Welcome to JankyScroll.js .

Let's say you have a poll. Your customers don't know to scroll to the bottom of the poll to submit the whole poll, and frequently fall into the hole of not submitting the whole poll.

That's not the goal! Take control of the scroll with jankyscroll.js! Don't just let them stroll through the poll - control the scroll. Don't let the scroll take it's toll.

<script src="jankyscroll.js"></script>

The sole goal of any poll is to enroll the whole soul in the role of poll patrol. To properly patrol the poll, you have to cajole a soul into scrolling through the whole poll. How can a soul be cajoled into scrolling a poll if the poll to be strolled isn't whole? To patrol this goal's role, we extol the virtues of jankyscroll.

It's important to indicate to users that there's more content on the page than meets the eye. That's why jankyscroll stutters randomly. It's on purpose.
